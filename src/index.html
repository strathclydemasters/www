The Strathclyde Masters is the premier basketball tournament in Scotland for men over 40, 50, 60, 65 and women over 40 or 50

{% comment %} {% endcomment %}

This is a small tournament with a maximum of 6/8 teams in each section. Each team will get a minimum of 3 games with finalists get 4 games. Expressing an interest does not guarantee you entry to tournament, and you will be notified if your team has been successful. As long as a player is at least 40, 50, 60 or 65 before the end of the year {{ site.season }} then they can play in the appropriate age group. The cost of the tournament is yet to be confirmed. All tournament games will be played in the one venue, The Lagoon Leisure Centre. This will necessitate some games being played on the Friday evening. In your expression of interest please indicate your availability to play a Friday evening game.
